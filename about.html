Nice to meet you!

I'm Amy. I'm currently pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Science focused on Intelligent Robotics at the University of Southern California.

My Background

In my free time, I like to work on Android apps for personal uses and, more recently, on web-development projects.

Personal Interests
