How to Reinstall Ubuntu From Grub Rescue

Embarassingly enough, I recently willy nilly deleted my ubuntu partition which left me stuck in GRUB rescue mode. Most of the tutorials I found assumed the reader had a bit more knowledge that I did, so fixing my computer took a lot of digging. Anyway, here's how to get your OS back if you're in the same boat.

Step 1: Create a bootable USB or CD of your desired OS

Here's a link to the resource I used to do this: http://www.pendrivelinux.com/universal-usb-installer-easy-as-1-2-3/

Step 2: Plug In and Reboot

Plug in your new bootable USB drive and reboot your computer

Step 3: Get out of GRUB rescue!

This was the really confusing part for me. I plugged in my bootable USB but still was unable to get out of the GRUB rescue loop when I powered down and rebooted. But... the internet was there to help. Here's what to do to get out:

- When your computer reboots, press the escape key

- Then type "exit"

- Then hit enter and install your OS!
